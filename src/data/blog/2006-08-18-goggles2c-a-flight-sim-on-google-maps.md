[![](/images/WindowsLiveWriter/GogglesaflightsimonGoogleMaps_8CD4/goggles_thumb%5B3%5D.jpg)](/images/WindowsLiveWriter/GogglesaflightsimonGoogleMaps_8CD4/goggles%5B3%5D.jpg) You have probably already seen over a hundred uses for Google Maps. Today, I received a link of a new use: [Goggles](http://www.isoma.net/games/goggles.html). Goggles allows you to fly a plane over a city, to climb and descent, to shoot at the scenery (I tried this over Amsterdam but didn't hear anything on the news. It's safe!). You even can crash.

A while ago I thought on developing a game on Google Maps too. No action-game, no flight simulator, but more a strategic game. Imagine a sort of Risk or Transport Tycoon on satellite imagery. Unfortunately, I didn't find the time to even think about a nice game concept. Maybe once!
