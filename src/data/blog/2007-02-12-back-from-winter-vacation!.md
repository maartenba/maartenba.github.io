Found a cheap (and good!) hotel over there, [Pension Haid](http://www.hotel-haid-pitztal.at/), which offered me breakfast and dinner for 36 EUR per night. I've been skiing on [Rifflsee and the Pitztaler Gletscher](http://www.pitztaler-gletscher.at), which offers great slopes (and also good weather conditions last week). Après-ski was offered by [Siglu](http://www.siglu.com/), which offered great [Kaiser Pilsner](http://www.kaiserbier.at/)!

Back to reality (and work) now, problem with all vacations is: they are too short!
