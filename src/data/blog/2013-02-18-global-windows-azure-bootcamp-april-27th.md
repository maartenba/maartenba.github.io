On April 27th, 2013, you’ll have the ability to join a Windows Azure Bootcamp on a location close to you. We’ve started this with the idea of maybe having 10 or 15 locations world wide. We were wrong. Here’s what happened:

[![](/images/image_thumb_221.png)](/images/image_259.png)

In short: we now have over 50 locations available where a bootcamp will be organized! This one day deep dive class will get you up to speed on developing for Windows Azure. The class includes a trainer with deep real world experience with Windows Azure, as well as a series of labs so you can practice what you just learned. It’s free, so [find your location](http://globalwindowsazure.azurewebsites.net/?page_id=151) and join the fun!
