[![](/images/logo_1.jpg)](http://www.theloungenet.com) Last week, I received my invitation to the [Lounge](http://www.theloungenet.com) network, an exclusive advertising network for “trusted and respected publishers focused on Microsoft technologies”. First of all, it’s cool to see that at least someone considers me as trusted and respected. Next, it’s probably a better advertising solution than the [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com) ads I had earlier. The Lounge will always display ads related to .NET technology, whereas Amazon had its days of displaying lamps and home furniture enhancements.

Let’s see how this works out.
